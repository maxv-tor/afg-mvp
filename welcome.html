Welcome to the Funding Application Portal

Before you begin, please take a moment to review the requirements.

As part of our Know Your Customer (KYC) policy, all applicants must provide scanned copies of certain identification and business documents.

This is a regulatory requirement designed to protect both you and our institution.

1. Documents you may be asked to provide

1.1. Personal Identification
  • Valid passport, or
  • Government-issued ID card, or
  • Driver’s license
1.2. Proof of Address
  • Recent utility bill, or
  • Bank statement, or
  • Official government correspondence (issued within the last 3 months)
1.3. Business Documentation (for companies)
  • Certificate of Incorporation / Business Registration
  • Articles of Association or Partnership Agreement
  • Tax Identification Number (TIN) or relevant tax certificate
  • Recent financial statements or bank reference (where applicable)

Important 2. Important Notice

If you are not ready to provide these documents, we strongly recommend that you do not start filling in the application form yet.

Your application cannot be processed or reviewed until the required documentation is submitted.

3. By preparing your documents in advance, you will

  1. Ensure a smooth and faster application process
  2. Avoid unnecessary delays or interruptions
  3. Increase the likelihood of timely approval
